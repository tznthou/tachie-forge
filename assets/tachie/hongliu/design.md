# 紅榴 (Hongliu) — Design Doc

## Identity
- Role: 女主角
- Personality: 表面與世無爭、呆萌天然，實則有驚人的觀察力與智慧
- Age range: 青春期，約 16-18 歲

## Visual Design
- Art style: anime_illustration
- Framing: waist-up
- Hair: 黑藍色（深黑帶藍光澤），雙馬尾，髮量豐厚，馬尾用黑色蝴蝶結髮飾固定
- Eyes: 深紅色（呼應「紅榴」之名），圓潤大眼，眼神帶一絲慵懶
- Build: 嬌小可愛
- Outfit: 地雷系 (jirai-kei) — 黑色為主搭配粉紅色點綴，蕾絲領口上衣，黑色蝴蝶結配飾，十字架項鍊，袖口有蕾絲裝飾
- Distinguishing features: 黑藍雙馬尾配深紅瞳孔的反差感，嬌小身形搭配暗黑可愛的地雷系風格
- Mood/aura: 表面柔軟無害、帶點慵懶的氣息；暗藏銳利

## Prompt Anchor

A petite young anime girl with dark blue-black twin tails held by black ribbon bows, deep crimson red eyes with round soft shape and a slightly sleepy gaze. She wears a jirai-kei (landmine girl) outfit: a black blouse with pink lace trim at the collar and cuffs, small black bows as accents, and a silver cross necklace. Her expression conveys a gentle, unassuming aura. She has a small, delicate frame. Hair strands and clothing edges should not form a closed silhouette — leave visible gaps between hair and outfit so the background is clearly separable.
