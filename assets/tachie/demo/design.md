# Demo — Design Doc

## Identity
- Role: README 示範用角色（非劇情角色）
- Personality: 開朗、親切
- Age range: 高中生, 約 15-17 歲

## Visual Design
- Art style: anime_illustration
- Framing: waist-up
- Hair: 黑色短髮, 齊瀏海鮑伯頭
- Eyes: 暖棕色, 溫和眼神
- Build: 中等身形
- Outfit: 簡單白色襯衫 + 深藍色開襟衫, 無配件
- Distinguishing features: 髮型俐落簡單, 刻意避開雙馬尾/蝴蝶結/蕾絲等容易形成封閉輪廓的元素
- Mood/aura: 友善、平易近人

## Prompt Anchor

A young anime girl with short black bob-cut hair and straight bangs, warm brown eyes with a gentle expression. She wears a simple white blouse with a plain collar under a navy cardigan, no accessories. Her expression conveys a friendly, approachable aura. Hair strands and clothing edges should not form a closed silhouette — leave visible gaps between hair and outfit so the background is clearly separable. No flyaway hair strands, no stray loose hair strands separated from the main hair silhouette, no light-colored highlight strokes floating outside the hair outline — keep the hair silhouette clean and contained.
